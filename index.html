ABOUT ME

I graduated as a Film and Sound Teacher at the National University of La Plata in Argentina. There, I also completed the Audiovisual Arts course of study, specializing in screenwriting.
I am currently employed by ISET 812 in the Superior Technician of Clothing and Textile Products Manufacturing. There, I'm teacher of Information and communications technologies.

I am also a freelancer Screenwriter. I have participated in different projects for some of the INCAA contests. including the 12th Federal Contest for the Development of Feature Film Projects ”Raymundo Gleyzer” 2020 , the Federal Short Series Development Contest 2020 for TV and other media, the Federal Competition for the Development of Unpublished Projects 2020 and the National Competition for the Production of Video Minutes of Thematic Animation "The Confinement" 2020.

My level of knowledge of the Javascript programming language is basic and I have working knowledge of HTML and CSS. Futhermore, I am also skilled at editing image and video due of my academic formation. I'm always practicing and trying to improve my skills.

Finally, I speak Spanish (as my mother tongue) and English at an intermediate written level.
